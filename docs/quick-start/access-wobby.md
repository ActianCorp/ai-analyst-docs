# Access Actian AI Analyst

To use Actian AI Analyst, you need to create an account. Here's how you can get started:

**Sign Up**&#x20;

1. Go to [https://www.wobby.ai/](https://www.wobby.ai/).
2. Click **Get started for free**.
3. Enter your details to create an account.
4.  When prompted, specify your organisation name.



**Sign In**&#x20;

* If you already have an account, go to [https://www.wobby.ai/](https://www.wobby.ai/) and click **Sign in**.
* Enter your email and password to access your organisation.

That's it!
